FAQ

What skins do you sell and buy?

▼

On our platform, you can buy and sell skins for CS2, Volorant. The price of skins is determined automatically. If you are selling a rare item and the price is not set automatically, please contact our technical support to agree on the price.

What determines the price?

▼

The price is calculated based on how much the item is worth on marketplaces for selling skins for real money. We do not base it on the Steam Market price.

What skins can be traded?

▼

All skins, including rare and exclusive ones.

How can I contact support?

▼

Please visit the "Contact Us" page, where you can reach out to our support team.

How long does it take for support to respond?

▼

Usually within 15 minutes.

Why can not the site see my skins?

▼

Try refreshing the site or waiting about 5 minutes in cases of high site traffic.

Are games other than CS supported?

▼

Yes, Valorant is also supported. In the future, we plan to add Dota 2, Overwatch 2, Rust, and League of Legends.
